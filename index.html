About this project

Built to answer where revenue is leaking.

2,400 accounts 71K subscriptions 1.3M events OpenView calibrated

Every B2B SaaS CFO and CRO faces the same tension: the business is growing, but revenue is leaking silently. PulseMetrics simulates a €128M ARR EMEA-focused SaaS company and builds the full analytical stack to surface where risk is hiding from the SMB churn rate sitting below benchmark to the Mid-Market LTV:CAC that's drifted under 3×.

All data is synthetic and calibrated to OpenView SaaS Benchmarks 2024, the Bessemer Venture Partners Cloud Index, and publicly disclosed metrics from Intercom and Teamwork-tier Irish B2B SaaS companies. The pipeline runs 14 analytical stages in Python with a logistic regression churn model achieving AUC 0.781.
